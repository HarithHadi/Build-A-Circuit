# ⚡ Build-A-Circuit
 
![Build-A-Circuit](/public/readme.png)
 
> An interactive web-based platform that makes learning electronics fun, safe, and beginner-friendly.
 
---
 
## 🔌 What is this?
 
**Build-A-Circuit** is a drag-and-drop circuit simulator built for students who are tired of staring at static textbook diagrams. Wire up components, see real-time simulation feedback, and actually *understand* what voltage, current, and resistance are doing — no lab coat required.
 
---
 
## ✨ Features
 
- 🧩 **Drag-and-drop circuit builder** — place and connect components intuitively
- ⚡ **Real-time simulation** — see what happens the moment you close a circuit
- 📚 **Guided lessons** — learn concepts step by step at your own pace
- 🧠 **Quizzes & challenges** — test your understanding with embedded assessments
- 💻 **Browser-based** — no installs, works on desktop and tablet
